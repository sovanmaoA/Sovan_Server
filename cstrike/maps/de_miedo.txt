de_miedo (Bomb/Defuse)
Map created by m3owth [for the 2016 Waterpark Contest]

Description:
The terrorists are planning to detonate a well-known waterpark to show their disagreement to the Spanish government. They already breached the area and searched for available bomb spots.
Luckily, there's no casualties reported.

Terrorists- Arm the bomb to one of the bombsites in the area. Take out all enemies on sight.

Counter-terrorists- Stop their plans and neutralize the opposing force as needed.

Note: Watch your surroundings, as the map is small to be explored by others.

Additional Note: There are two bomb spots in the mission, one in the stairs area just near the balcony. The other ones is in the pipes inside the restricted area.
